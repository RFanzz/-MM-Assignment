Introduction

Sekolah@MMU is a charity program that started in 2003, with the core objective to expose secondary school students to the importance of Information Technology & Multimedia knowledge. In 2013, Sekretariat Sekolah@MMU has been rebranded into a digital volunteerism platform that focuses on skills needed in Digital Technology, Entrepreneurship, and Digital Innovation.

The main objective is to support the government to upskill our future generations in areas of identified skills such as Digital Technology, Entrepreneurship, and Digital Innovation. Aside from that, this would be a platform to supercharge the talent by practicing industry-ready skills via a project management framework.

Directly report under the Student Lifestyle & Experience (STyLE) of Multimedia University, Sekretariat Sekolah@MMU is a non-profit organization that starts with zero capital fund upfront annually. For that reason, Sekretariat Sekolah@MMU always looks forward to any smart partnership opportunities to support our agenda throughout the year.

Objective

The objective of Sekretariat Sekolah@MMU is to advocate all schools in Malaysia as MMU’s foster schools. Thus, the program will act as a centre of empowerment for the nation’s future to become a top 20 nation in economic development, social advancement and innovation.

Vision

To be a premier student society for community that propagates the generation and dissemination of knowledge in cutting edge technologies in line with MMU’s vision to be a top 100 University in Asia with Global Recognition by 2020.
